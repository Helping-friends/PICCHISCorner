2. Information We Collect

At Picchi's Corner, we collect different types of information from users who interact with our website, services, or make purchases. This information allows us to improve your experience, fulfill orders, and provide relevant content. Here, we detail the types of information we collect.

2.1 Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify you, either directly or indirectly. We collect the following personal information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Contact Information: This includes your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and any other information you provide to communicate with us.
  • Payment Information: This includes your credit card details, PayPal account, or other financial information used to process transactions on our website.
  • Account Information: This includes your username, password, account settings, and security questions used to authenticate your identity.
  • Transaction Data: Information about the purchases you make, such as the products, quantities, prices, and payment methods.
  • Shipping Information: Your address, delivery preferences, and any additional information needed to process and ship your orders.
  • Email Correspondence: Any emails, messages, or communications you send to us, including inquiries, feedback, and complaints.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information is data that cannot be used to identify you directly. We collect non-personal data to help us improve the website and services. The following types of non-personal data are collected:

  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you are using to access our site, including the type of device, browser version, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • Usage Data: This includes details about how you use the website, such as pages viewed, time spent on the site, and interactions with elements on the page.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about how visitors navigate our site, what content they engage with, and for advertising purposes.

2.3 Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political opinions, or health information, unless it is necessary for processing a specific order or fulfilling legal obligations. In the case that we do need to collect sensitive data, we will explicitly ask for your consent.
